FAMOUS TOURIST SPOTS IN THE PHILIPPINES

The country Philippines is known for owning one of the most attractive tourist destinations. Philippines is famous mostly for its beautiful beaches, mountains, and rich heritage, being given the title as Asia's Pearl of the Orient. Not only do the eye-catching locations and natural beauty of the Philippines help attract tourists, but so does the hospitality, friendliness, and kindness of the Filipinos that foreigners surely love coming back to. People love having a taste of delicious Filipino food; such as Adobo, Crispy Pata, Pancit, and more!

Here are four of the most popular destinations in the Philippines!


SIARGAO
  • Siargao is an island in Surigao del Norte.
  • It is mainly known for introducing Surfing to the country, claiming the title, "Surfing Capital of the Philippines". Though, it also provides more such as rock climbing, tree swinging, and more adventurous activities.
  • Siargao is great for adventurous people as it contains natural reserves such as the Sugba Lagoon, Sohoton Cove, and Magpupungko Rock Pool which allows people to express and reveal their adventurous sides!

BAGUIO
  • Baguio is within Benguet, inside the island of Luzon.
  • It is known as the City of Pines, and it was also given the title, "Summer Capital of the Philippines".
  • Baguio is well known for its parks such as the Burnham Park which offers plentiful of activities; like rowing a boat, playing in a children's playground, and many more that may be enjoyed by a family.
  • The prominent and popular Strawberry Farm resides in Baguio. It is said that the best months for strawberry picking are April, March, and May. Expect very tasteful and fresh strawberry flavored products here!

PALAWAN
  • Palawan is a province located in the region of Mimaropa.
  • It is known as the Philippines' "Last ecological frontier".
  • People love coming back to Palawan due to its beautiful scenery of the bodies of water. Those who love swimming, diving, and any other water activities are recommended to visit Palawan!
  • Popular destinations such as Puerto Princesa, Coron, and El Nido can be found within Palawan. Palawan offers activities such as Scuba diving, Snorkeling, Kayaking, even Ziplining and Rock climbing!

BOHOL
  • Bohol is an island province found in the Central Visayas region of the country.
  • It is known as the "Jewel of the Philippines".
  • The famous Chocolate Hills is one of the reasons why tourists love going to Bohol! It is regarded as one of the most attractive tourist spots in the country.
  • You may also visit other places and do fun activities! Bohol has places such as the Bohol Tarsier Conservation Area where you may look at tarsiers, as tarsiers are indeed endangered species and can only be found in few locations, and one of them is the Philippines! It is also recommended to visit Bohol's beaches and adventure parks!
